About Me

I've always had a passion for tech. From building custom pc's in my youth to running a business deploying surveillance equipment after college, cutting edge technology has been where my heart is.

More recently, however, I've gravitated towards the software end of things and fell in love with the world of web development. I find it particularly fulfilling to take a concept or design out of the imagination and turn it into new software. It's fun to create!

I also have been trading stocks and foreign exchange for the past several years now, so most of my time outside of coding is spent studying financial markets or watching or playing sports for recreation and fitness. It's great mentally to rotate and switch gears with different tasks to remain fresh and sharp within each.

I'm eager, and thanks to Thinkful, ready to deliver quality software and web applications utilizing Javascript, React, Node.JS, MongoDB and other databases. I'm looking forward to working with future clients and especially fellow developers on exciting and challenging new projects.
